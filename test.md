To lose fat in two months effectively, focus on a combination of strength training, cardiovascular exercise, and proper nutrition. Here’s how you can structure your routine:

### Strength Training (2-3 times/week)
1. **Compound Movements:**
   - **Legs:** Squats or Deadlifts.
   - **Glutes:** Lunges or Glute Bridges.
   - **Chest:** Bench Press or Incline Dumbbell Press.
   - **Shoulders:** Overhead Press or Lateral Raises.
   - **Triceps:** Pushdowns or Skull Crushers.
   - **Biceps:** Bicep Curls or Hammer Curls.
   
   These movements target multiple muscle groups simultaneously, promoting overall strength gains while burning calories.

2. **Cardiovascular Exercise:**
   - Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio weekly. This could include activities like running, cycling, swimming, or brisk walking.

### Nutrition Plan (Daily)
1. **Caloric Intake:**
   - Calculate your daily caloric needs based on your age, sex, weight, and activity level. For example, a sedentary adult male might need around 2,000-2,500 calories per day.
   - Reduce calorie intake by 500-750 calories below your maintenance level each week to promote fat loss.

2. **Macronutrient Distribution:**
   - **Protein:** Ensure you consume enough protein to support muscle recovery and growth. Aim for 1.2-2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight.
     - Example: If you weigh 80 kg, aim for 96-176 grams of protein per day.
   - **Carbohydrates:** Focus on complex carbs from whole foods like vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.
   - **Fats:** Include healthy fats such as avocados, nuts
